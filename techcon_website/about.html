History

Founded in 2020, TechCon began as a small gathering of student innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated conferences for young builders across Africa, featuring workshops, hackathons, and talks from world-class experts.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of engineers and entrepreneurs by providing hands-on learning opportunities, mentorship, and a platform to showcase real solutions to global challenges.
